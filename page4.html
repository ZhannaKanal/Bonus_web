About Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is a young and independent state. Our country grows and gets stronger every year. Fifteen million people and more than a hundred nationalities and ethnic groups live in amicable ways in Kazakhstan. The people of Kazakhstan are very friendly, hospitable and generous.
